Краткое названиеA Study to Evaluate Safety of Long Term Therapy of Certolizumab Pegol Patients With Crohn's Disease
Полное названиеA Phase IIIb, Open-label, follow-on Trial to C87085 Designed to Assess the Long-term Safety of Certolizumab Pegol, in Subjects With Moderately to Severely Active Crohn's Disease Who Have Participated in Study C87085
